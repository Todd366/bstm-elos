# BSTM-PRIN-000 — "Marketing Fixes Every Business Problem"

**Status:** 🔴 REJECTED

**Reason:** Some businesses do not have visibility problems. They have supply problems, capital problems, or operational problems. Trial 006 (Jikisa) is direct disconfirming evidence — the business had strong word-of-mouth and consistent demand, and a marketing-first fix would have worsened its actual bottleneck (capital velocity).

**Superseded by:** `BSTM-PRIN-008` (The Visibility Illusion), which replaces the blanket assumption with a diagnostic boundary.
