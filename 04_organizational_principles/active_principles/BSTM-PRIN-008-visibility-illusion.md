# BSTM-PRIN-008 — The Visibility Illusion

**Status:** 🟢 ACTIVE

**Principle:** Do not increase customer demand when supply capacity (restocking/capital velocity) is the actual bottleneck.

**Evidence:** Trial BSTM-100T-006 — Jikisa. Terminal data proved capital constraints, not customer exposure, were the limiting factor. Marketing spend would have accelerated stockouts and damaged community trust.

**Boundary:** Only applies when inventory/capital velocity is the confirmed limiting factor (verify via terminal/POS data, not sentiment or word-of-mouth signals — those can look identical to an Exposure problem).

**Confidence Profile:**
- Empirical Weight: 3/5 (single strong trial, high-quality terminal evidence)
- Context Breadth: 2/5 (confirmed in multi-line retail only so far)
- Operational Reliability: 4/5 (directly prevented a costly misallocation)
- Longevity Horizon: 4/5 (capital-velocity dynamics are structural, not trend-dependent)
