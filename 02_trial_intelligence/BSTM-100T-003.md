## Context
**Correction on record:** Nado is not a standard clothing shop — it is a fully mobile, vehicle-based retail operation. All transactions are executed directly out of a transport vehicle to eliminate fixed real estate overhead entirely. This reclassifies the business from ordinary Retail into the Agility archetype (see `03_pattern_intelligence/archetype-agility-type.md`).

## Assumption Registry
- **Assumption:** Mobile operations can substitute structural real estate while maintaining system coordination.
- Initial Confidence: 4/5

## Hypothesis
A business does not require a permanent storefront to scale, provided digital communication channels preserve customer reach across shifting locations.

## Experiment
Deployed an online inventory showcase channel combined with localized WhatsApp community updates to track the vehicle's position and stock.

## Raw Evidence
The node shifted position across 3 different physical areas over 24 hours while maintaining a consistent baseline transaction level. No physical storefront was used at any point.

## Interpretation
Mobility minimizes overhead and maximizes structural adaptability, but it introduces three specific system requirements that a fixed retailer does not face:
1. **Inventory tracking** across changing locations
2. **Customer communication** (where is the vehicle right now?)
3. **Product showcasing** substituting for a walk-in display

## Confidence Update
- Initial: 4/5
- Final: 5/5
- Reason for change: Real-world operational viability proven across multiple shifting physical locations without loss of transaction volume.

## Error Digestion
Zero structural fractures logged.

## Capability Check
- Knowledge gained: Yes.
- New capability created: Yes — validated a low-overhead, mobile-retail system blueprint, directly reusable for future BSTM mobile-commerce nodes (CabLink-adjacent vendors).

## BSTM Insight
A business can operate successfully without a physical storefront, but technology can give it the visibility and structure of a much bigger business — provided location, inventory, and communication are synchronized in real time.
