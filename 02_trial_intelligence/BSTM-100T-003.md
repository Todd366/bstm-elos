# Hypothesis

A business does not require a permanent storefront to scale, provided digital communication channels preserve customer reach across shifting locations.

# Experiment

Deployed an online inventory showcase channel combined with localized WhatsApp community updates to track vehicle position and available stock.

## Raw Evidence

The business shifted position across three different physical areas within 24 hours while maintaining consistent transaction activity.

No permanent storefront was used during operations.

## Interpretation

Mobility reduces overhead and increases structural adaptability.

However, mobile retail creates three additional system requirements:

1. Inventory tracking across changing locations.
2. Customer communication regarding current location.
3. Product showcasing to replace storefront visibility.
