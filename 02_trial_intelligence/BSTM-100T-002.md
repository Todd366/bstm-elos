## Raw Evidence
No structured field checklist was completed this trial (data-quality gap, logged above). Interview evidence: the owner explicitly named two distinct slowdown causes — winter seasonality and price-sensitive customers defecting to cheaper salons — not visibility alone. ## Interpretation
This trial still confirms the Exposure archetype (marketing/booking infrastructure is weak — no clear online booking, inconsistent channel strategy), but it also surfaces two compounding, non-exposure factors: seasonal demand cycles and price competition. Treating this purely as an Exposure-Type fix (more marketing) would leave the price-competition and seasonality pressure unaddressed.
