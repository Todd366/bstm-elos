A single localized footprint running four services simultaneously: tuckshop, mini-catering, car wash, and internet café.

## Assumption Registry
- **Assumption:** Divergent service lines can share operational capacity without creating structural data-log confusion.

## Hypothesis
Unifying different service channels under a single operational management framework will optimize cross-selling efficiency.

## Experiment

## Raw Evidence

## Interpretation
Multi-service setups are not simple operations — they are complex ecosystems that require synchronized accounting to prevent profitable units from silently subsidizing decaying branches.
