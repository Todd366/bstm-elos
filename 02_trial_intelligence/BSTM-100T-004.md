## Assumption Registry

- Initial Confidence: 3/5

## Hypothesis
Introducing digital stock trackers will immediately minimize inventory decay and out-of-stock occurrences.

## Experiment
Introduced a baseline digital inventory tracking sheet to the legacy operator.

## Raw Evidence
Operator logged data for the first 6 hours, then reverted entirely to manual cash-drawer sorting by hour 12.

## Interpretation
Legacy operations carry heavy behavioral momentum. Technical systems must adapt to human habits rather than forcing abrupt operational change — modernization needs a transition ramp, not a swap.

## Confidence Update
- Initial: 3/5
- Final: 2/5
- Reason for change: Observed direct behavioral resistance to the tracking tool.
