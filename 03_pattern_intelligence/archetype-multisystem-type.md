# Archetype: Multi-System Type

**Definition:** Multiple business operations exist under one roof or one operator, but lack synchronization — profitable lines silently subsidize decaying ones.

**Evidence:**
- Trial 004 — 20-Year-Old Tuckshop (legacy single-system, resisted digitization — borderline case)
- Trial 005 — NDS (tuckshop + catering + car wash + internet café — cross-subsidization confirmed)

**Diagnostic signature:** Shared cash drawer or shared physical space across functionally distinct revenue lines; no per-line accounting; operator can describe overall health but not per-line health.

**Required intervention:** Synchronized, per-line accounting (FlowLedger / Room 17) before any growth intervention — you cannot fix what you cannot separate.

**Evolution state:** 🟢 Active — 2 confirming trials (1 partial/inconclusive, 1 confirmed).
