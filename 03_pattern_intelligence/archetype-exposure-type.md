# Archetype: Exposure Type

**Definition:** A good product or service exists but lacks visibility, reach, or customer awareness.

**Evidence:**
- Trial 001 — Jewelry Store (45 profile views in 48hrs post-listing)
- Trial 002 — Hawkers Beauty Spa (4-day wait → fully booked in 72hrs)

**Diagnostic signature:** Quality inventory/skill is present; digital or physical footprint is near-zero; a single visibility intervention produces immediate, measurable volume change.

**Boundary:** Only applies when the operator can already fulfill demand once it arrives — see the counter-case in Trial 006, where treating a Liquidity problem as Exposure would have been actively harmful (`BSTM-PRIN-008`).

**Evolution state:** 🟢 Active — 2 confirming trials, no disconfirming evidence yet.
