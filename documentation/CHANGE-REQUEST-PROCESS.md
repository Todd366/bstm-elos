# Blueprint Change Request Process

The Master Blueprint (`README.md`) is frozen at v1.0. It can only change through this process:

1. **Evidence** — cite the specific trial(s) in `02_trial_intelligence/` that expose the limitation.
2. **Limitation** — state in one paragraph what the current blueprint fails to handle.
3. **Proposed Change** — the specific section, rule, or template to modify.
4. **Approval** — reviewed by the Founder & Field Intelligence Operator (Todd). AI may draft the proposal but cannot approve it (see Section 10, Human-AI-System Relationship).
5. **Version Bump** — `v1.x` for templates/clarifications/metrics/examples; `v2.0` for architecture/philosophy/fundamental restructuring.

No idea automatically becomes architecture. Reality must approve it first.
